About Me

Sachin Tandon



Sachin works to help businesses in the “Media” & “Technology” sectors grow, prosper, and thrive! He does this current through Science & Engineering (e.g. Data Engineering, Data Analytics, Data Science, and other Web-based tools & technologies), so businesses can get the insight they need to make better business decisions!

Sachin has been fortunate enough in his 14+ year career so far to have worked with a wide range of people, across a variety of countries, including the U.K., U.S.A., India, Middle East, and Japan!

In fact, it was after learning Japanese, at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, whilst doing his Master’s Degree, that Sachin, in 2007, went to Tokyo to work for the Technology company, Hitachi Digital (where he worked with Hitachi Engineers to solve a challenging Manufacturing Engineering problem). It was when he was in Tokyo, Japan, that he also bought his first Digital SLR Camera from the electronics district, Akihabara, so he could practice his Photography but also travel the country, to deepen his culturing understanding of Japanese food, history, heritage and tradition.

After completing his Engineering degree from the University of Cambridge, U.K (where Sachin was also President of the College Film Society - Caius Films), Sachin then set out to understand and learn more about Finance, and worked at the Professional Services company, PwC, in London, U.K. There, whilst he worked for clients by day during the week, he also studied to become a qualified Accountant (and, after much graft, after three years successfully also became a Chartered Accountant!)

After his early years in Finance, Sachin then went on to pursue a self-funded MBA, from Manchester Business School, U.K., to learn more widely about business - specifically of areas such as: Marketing, Human Resources, Innovation & Entrepreneurship, Business Strategy, Leadership, Management, International & Digital Commerce, and then within Finance, areas such as: Corporate Finance, Mergers & Acquisitions, and Venture Capital. It was an experience that gave him a much wider perspective of business, and also allowed him to further develop his management and leadership skills.

After completing his MBA, Sachin then decided to see what it might be like to work through his own company. So he set up the venture: StarTech Enterprises - a Ltd Co. vehicle through which he works passionately to help “Media” & “Technology” companies grow and develop themselves, so they can better serve both their employees and customers. He does this, currently, by providing input and challenge to companies and individual leaders within them, to try and pilot new ideas, products, and technologies - so they can experiment with new things, re-invent themselves a little, and push the boundaries a little further!

Sachin has now, through his career to date, been able to work for companies ranging from: BBC Media, Hitachi Digital, Jaguar Land Rover, WPP (Wunderman Thompson, Hogarth, Geometry), The Omnicom Media Group - in the Media, Entertainment, & Technology sectors - to then Diageo Corporate Ventures, The Landmark Group, Happy Ratio, and J L Rohatgi Memorial Eye Hospital - in the Retail, Food, Drinks, and Medical sectors.

And in so doing, Sachin has been able to accumulate a good amount of experience and knowledge of the Media, Technology, Marketing, Retail, Finance, Education, and Travel industries.

Having established an extensive network of contacts, Sachin’s primary focus now, is on giving his clients the best possible ideas and insights, through lasting and practical solutions, so they can continue to flourish and expand!

The Day to Day?



Day to day, Sachin works to:

  • Develop and refine his venture's offering, whilst networking & building relationships with key organisations in the “Media” & “Technology” sectors, surveying and understanding the needs of the market

  • Meet, pro-actively, with potential clients to build an extensive network of contacts, and better understand how solutions can be developed to fulfil the sector's most pressing needs and issues

  • Conduct cross-disciplinary research, across areas of technology, marketing, advertising, business development, human resource management, advanced analytics, sustainability, and financing - to keep pace with evolving customer needs

Outside Interests



When he is not helping “Media” & “Technology” companies become better at what they do, Sachin continues to lead a bright and colorful life. This includes enjoying his recent travels to remote parts of Southern Asia, the Far East, Southern Africa, Western, and Eastern Europe - using the opportunity to absorb new cultures, meet new people, and really savour the diversity of life!

When not working, you can probably find him playing tennis, watching cricket, Formula 1 motor sport, or following the progress of his favourite football team - Mancheser United (His parents lived not very far from Old Trafford, before he was born!)

He continues to strongly pursue his passions for film, media, entertainment, engineering, and technology, reading books on the topic (like: The Wired, M.I.T. Tech Review, Total Film Magazine, and National Geographic !), whilst also celebrating the remarkable things other people have done in the field - in addition - to participating in a wide range of cultural and gastronomic activities... when he gets the time!

So, life, all round, is very busy!
